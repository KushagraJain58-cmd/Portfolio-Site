Kagzi Khayal

What's Inside

In today's era, with rapid digitalization and the trend of watching movies and web series, reading books has been lagging behind, especially Hindi books which have been losing its essence with time.

To rejuvenate the love for Hindi poetry, Kagzi Khayal is an initiative to bring people closer to their mother language. It's a collection of beautiful poems by different poets who have showcased their varied experiences of love, separation, relationships, societal challenges and inspiring events through this book. These poems have been written with utmost honesty showing different aspects of life which will make you relate to those experiences and leave you spellbound.

Buy this book if you are a fan of Hindi poetry
