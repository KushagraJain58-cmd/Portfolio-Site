My Writing Journey

“Writing is like breathing to me. We don’t take out time for breathing, so does for writing”. Hey there! If there is one word that describes me well is, "Explorer". I am an explorer set out in this chaotic world with my crazy thoughts ready to accept any challenge that comes my way.
I am always open to new experiences and in search of new stories to pen down. So, maybe you got the idea that I am a writer. I love to write poems, stories, blogs and scripts. I started my writing journey during my under-graduation. It started with a spark, a sudden inspiration which motivated me to pen down a couple of shayaris like most of the people. But then, I started discovering this world more. Started reading poetry legends. Expanded it further to stories, novels, scripts and anything I could find in literature. Slowly, the secrets of this world started unraveling before me. With every poem and story, I grew more attached to this world, similar to falling in love, I guess.

Educational Background

I have been born and brought up in Delhi, did my schooling and graduation from here itself. In 12th class, my good percentage landed up me in North Campus, Delhi University pursuing Economics Hons. a dream of every commerce student. But soon enough, I realised that I am not made for this field. Continuously failing in all the MA (Economics) entrance exams, I did not get admission in any college. With shackled confidence and nowhere to go to, I started applying for jobs. But destiny had something else stored for me.

Rather than dealing with numbers and data anymore, I chose to go into creative field. That’s when, I took admission in MA (PR & Event Management) in Amity University in 2018. I unleashed my potential there and enhanced my skills in communication, management, radio and much more.

What I Love!!

  • I love to meet and interact with new people. I am always eager to learn. I love public speaking and am a proud member of Delhi Toastmasters.
  • I am also appointed as Chief Secretary in JITO (Jain International Trade Organization), Youth Wing.
  • I love audio industry and run my podcast on Spotify and other podcast apps
  • Other than this, I have an immense inclination for theatre, improvisational theatre, travelling and cycling
