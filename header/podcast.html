My Podcast Journey

My journey in audio industry began with Radio Amity 107.8 FM, a community radio station in Amity University during my Masters. There I found my passion, my voice. Conducting interviews with numerous guests, speaking on various topics like sports, mental health, fitness, and much more made me fall in love with audio industry. It led me to starting my own podcast in 2020, named "Soul's Tune". I have also worked as Podcast host with Inkfeathers Publishing and helped in kickstart their podcast.

I have given various workshops on " How to start a podcast" and am open to collaboration if you need a voice-over or podcast guidance. Please fill out your details given below to get in touch.
