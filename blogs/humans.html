Humans Are Adaptive!

We have seen superheroes on TV but we see one daily in our real life as well. No, it's not our parents, or some idol. We see superhero in the mirror. Yes, that's me, that's you! Why I am saying that is because we know how to adapt!

We have this superpower of adapting to any situation or any problem. First we are apprehensive that how we will adjust but when moment comes, we eventually do.

If we had a habit of running in the park then we started spot-jogging in our house during lockdown. If work from home got dull and tedious then we explored ways out and started working from gardens. Creative and unique solutions always lie under the umbrella of adaption.

We will crib about the problem for two days but then on third day, we would find ways to turn things around. We can't sit idle or anxious for long. Our mind starts running during a change. We dread it but we need it. We often hear people say 'Life is out there'. It doesn't mean that it's somewhere outside your house or somewhere in mountains, it's out of your comfort zone. The moment you step out of your comfort zone and adapt according to the situation, life is out there!
So, however the situation is, we have this superpower of adapting and we will do just fine.
